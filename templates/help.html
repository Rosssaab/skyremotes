Help & FAQ

Setting Up Your Sky Remote

Initial Setup

  1. Insert the batteries (provided) into your remote
  2. Ensure your Sky box is plugged in and turned on
  3. Point the remote at your Sky box
  4. Press any button to begin using

TV Control Setup

To control your TV's power and volume:

  1. Press and hold the TV button until the light flashes twice
  2. Enter your TV's brand code (see manual)
  3. Point remote at TV and press Power
  4. If TV responds, press Select to save

Troubleshooting

Common Issues

  1. Check batteries are inserted correctly
  2. Try new batteries
  3. Ensure nothing is blocking the signal
  4. Reset remote by removing batteries for 1 minute
